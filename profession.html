Point Cloud Generation

The most valued product for our clients

Drones have revolutionised a lot of ways in which many tasks are done, especially in the surveying world. They have made tasks safer, faster, and more profitable which are three facets that are very valuable to any business and so many companies have adopted this technology in order to stay competitive. Drones are being used to conduct critical infrastructure inspections using electro-optical sensors, high-zoom rgb cameras, infrared, and LiDAR technology. These different technologies are often not built into the drones and so a user must also purchase a payload that is attached to the bottom of the drone that has the desired ability. Above are snapshots of some data captured by our drones and have generated a 3D point cloud that can be manipulated and moved around in a 3D viewing software that can read LAS files. From here, clients often classify the LAS files and have the software differentiate between the buildings, grass and trees from the ground and strip out everything so that only the ground is showing. They can then create a surface from the remaining LAS which can be converted to a contour map that is given to civil engineers that then use that map for buiding new developments.
